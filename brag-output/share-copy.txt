──────────────────────────────────────────────
LINKEDIN
──────────────────────────────────────────────
Most meeting-notes tools ship your audio to someone else's cloud. Parley doesn't.

It's a self-hosted Discord bot: it joins your voice channel, transcribes every
speaker locally with faster-whisper, and posts AI-generated notes — TL;DR,
decisions, action items, and talk-time — into a Discord thread. A self-hosted web
dashboard keeps every meeting, action item, and full-text search in one place.
Pluggable summarizer (Gemini, any OpenAI endpoint, or fully-offline Ollama).

Open source, ISC-licensed, free. github.com/SakethKanchi/parley
